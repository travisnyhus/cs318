Gameshow

The Gameshow is the longest running show in the station's lineup. Episodes feature TV-10's own twist on classic games like "Wing Feud" and "Minute to Win it". In addition to in-studio filming, the show is also filmed on location all over campus.Join host Erin McManaway each Wednesday night channel on 10.1.
