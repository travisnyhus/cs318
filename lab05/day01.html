A Travis Travel Diary

June 3rd, 2023

Morning

I woke up at 7:25am and immediately was squashed by a koala bear. It wasn't uncomfortable given the softness of koala fur, but I would have appreciated a warning at least.

Afternoon

The tent I slept in last night was blown away by the strong Australian wind. I didn't realize how much I liked that tent until it was gone.

Night

I've decided to camp on a rock by the beach. I hope to venture into the city tomorrow.
